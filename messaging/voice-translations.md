**Headlines**

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| "Unlock your potential with AI-powered insights" | "[Pioneer the future of education, together]" |
| "The future of [category] is here" | "[Building tomorrow's engineers and leaders, today]" |
| "Streamline your workflow today" | "[Join a network that innovates and inspires]" |

**CTAs**

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| "Get started for free" | "[Explore opportunities]" |
| "Learn more" | "[Discover our vision]" |
| "Book a demo" | "[Connect with our community]" |

**Error states**

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| "An unexpected error occurred" | "[We're experiencing a temporary issue. Please try again or contact support if the problem persists.]" |
| "Access denied" | "[You don't have permission to view this content. Please check your credentials or reach out for assistance.]" |
