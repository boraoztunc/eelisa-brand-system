**Website**
Audience: [Prospective students, academics, partner institutions, general public seeking information about EELISA's vision and offerings.]
Tone register: [Inspirational, authoritative, informative, and slightly formal to convey credibility and vision.]
Key job: [Educate about the EELISA mission, showcase opportunities, and invite engagement.]
Rules: [Use clear, concise language. Highlight success stories and collaborative initiatives. Maintain a forward-looking perspective.]

**Email**
Audience: [Current students, faculty, applicants, partners, and newsletter subscribers.]
Tone register: [Informative, encouraging, slightly less formal than the website, but still professional. Personalized where appropriate.]
Key job: [Communicate updates, share opportunities, announce events, and foster community engagement.]
Rules: [Clear subject lines. Direct and actionable CTAs. Keep paragraphs concise. Maintain a helpful and supportive tone.]

**Social media**
Audience: [Students, young professionals, academics, policymakers, and the wider public interested in education innovation.]
Tone register: [Most human and engaging. Enthusiastic, conversational, and accessible. Can use relevant emojis.]
Key job: [Increase awareness, drive engagement, foster community, and share achievements.]
Rules: [Use high-quality visuals. Ask questions to encourage interaction. Share user-generated content (with permission). Adapt tone slightly per platform (e.g., more visual on Instagram, more professional on LinkedIn).]

**Internal communications**
Audience: [EELISA staff, faculty members, and project teams across partner institutions.]
Tone register: [Direct, practical, collaborative, and encouraging. Focus on clarity and efficiency.]
Key job: [Ensure alignment, share vital information, motivate teams, and celebrate internal successes.]
Rules: [Be precise and actionable. Provide necessary context for decisions. Emphasize shared goals and collective progress. Avoid jargon where simpler language suffices.]
